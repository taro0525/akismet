Important:
To use the release 2.0.3 of the plugin, you must have ZenPhoto greater than or equal to to version 1.4.4.

Download and unzip the file akismet_2.0.1.zip on your computer
Export all files in the /plugin directory of your ZenPhoto site
In the admin of ZenPhoto, go to plugins > spam and activate akismet plugin
Go to Akismet website to register and get a key
Go to options > plugin > akismet and provide your valide key
You can configure "forgive" option (suspected spam are marked for moderation)
